The Problem

Your AI agents are autonomous. Your security isn't keeping up.

Every major cloud provider has launched agent frameworks in the last 12 months. AI teams are shipping autonomous agents on AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud — handling transactions, accessing internal systems, and making decisions at scale.

98%
of enterprises deploying agentic AI have no security governance over what those agents actually do.

The blind spot your CSPM can't see

  • No visibility into agent actions at runtime — tool calls, data access, cross-agent communication
  • No audit trail. No way to detect prompt injection, data exfiltration, or privilege escalation across agent chains
  • Existing CSPM tools were built for cloud infrastructure — they cannot see what AI agents do
  • The board asks "Are our agents safe?" and security teams have no answer
